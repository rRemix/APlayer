<p><i>APlayer</i> is a beautiful and powerful music player. Next to other useful features you would expect from a musicplayer, it offers a.o.:</p><ul><li>Dynamic theme colors</li><li>Lyric support(include offline, online and embedded lyrics)</li><li>Homescreen widget</li><li>Desktop lyric</li><li>Custom album&artist&playlist images and auto download</li><li>Lockscreen playback controls</li><li>Custom playback rate</li><li>Equalizer(built-in & system)</li><li>Sleep Timer</li><li>Tag editor</li><li>Create, edit, import, export playlists</li></ul>
